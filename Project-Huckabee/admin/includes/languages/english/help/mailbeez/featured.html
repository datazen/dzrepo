Use your existing Featured Products data

You’ve already spent time assigning Featured Products, and handily, the Mailbeez Featured Products module takes your existing data, so you don’t need to spend any extra time on your email campaigns replicating existing data.

The result is less e-commerce administration, and more time getting on with the business of selling.
